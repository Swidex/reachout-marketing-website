About

FINDining is a revolutionary new app that solves the problem of not knowing where to eat and not being able to find new, local restaurants. FINDining's advanced restaurant recommendation algorithm uses your eating preferences and tastes in order to give you unique restaurant recommendations.

FINDining can also be used socially. Add your friends within the app to see what restaurants they liked and where they ate at. Get together with a group of friends to create a group within the app and receive restaurant recommendations for the entire group.


Features


Discover


Find new and local restaurants that align with your tastes and eating preferences.

Friends


Add friends within the app and discover which restaurants your friends like.

Groups
